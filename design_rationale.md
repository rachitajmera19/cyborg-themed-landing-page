# Design Rationale

## Why this approach is stronger

Most cyborg-theme pages become generic robot pages. This design focuses on the actual concept of a cyborg: a human enhanced by machine systems.

## Design Choices

- Dark futuristic background for cyber atmosphere
- Cyan and green accents to show active technology and bio signals
- Half-human, half-machine face to directly communicate the cyborg idea
- HUD cards to show augmented interface thinking
- Interactive mission dashboard to make the project feel alive
- Architecture section to add technical depth
- Responsive device section to clearly satisfy the task requirement

## What makes it submission-ready

- It is not dependent on paid images or external design assets.
- It works with just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- It has a clear story and can be explained confidently in a demo recording.
- It is clean enough for GitHub Pages hosting.
